Admission Process

We welcome students to join the Jack and Jill School family! Our admission process is simple and transparent to help parents enroll their children with ease.

Steps to Apply:

  • Visit the school office between 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM.
  • Collect and fill the admission form.
  • Submit required documents (listed below).
  • Attend a short interaction (for the child).
  • Wait for confirmation call or email from the school.

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate (photocopy)
  • 2 Passport Size Photos of the Student
  • Previous School Report Card (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card Copy (Student & Parent)

Age Criteria:

Nursery – Minimum 3 years
LKG – Minimum 4 years
UKG – Minimum 5 years
Class 1 onwards – As per previous school records

For further inquiries, please contact the school office or email us at admissions@jackandjillschool.in
